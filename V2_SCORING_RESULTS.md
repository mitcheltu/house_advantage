| Change | V1 | V2 |
|--------|----|----|

| `bill_proximity` | — | Days to nearest sector-matched bill |
| `amount_zscore` | — | Trade amount z-score within politician |
| `cluster_score` | — | Same-ticker ±7-day clustering count |
| Model 2 active features | 1 (cohort_alpha) | 2 (cohort_alpha + pre_trade_alpha) |

## Disclosure Lag Dominance — Eliminated

The primary goal of V2 was to fix the disclosure_lag dominance problem identified during V1 model validation.

| Metric | V1 | V2 |
|--------|----|----|
| Mean disclosure_lag (flagged trades) | ~2.6x higher than unflagged | ~0.98x (essentially equal) |
| % of detections attributable to disclosure_lag | ~61% | Balanced across all 9 features |

The `log1p` transform compressed the 0–365 day range into ~0–5.9 log-space, preventing extreme lag values from dominating IsolationForest tree splits.
